Gary Hillerson, M.A.

Bookbinding Projects

I've been practicing the fine craft of bookbinding since 1995. I've been a member of the Hand Bookbinder's Guild, and apprenticed with local bookbinding wizard Constance Hunter from 1999 til 2003.

I've designed numerous fine journals using various binding techniques, including Coptic bindings, library bindings, medieval-style flexible bindings, and case-bound bindings. I've also restored a number of fine, older books, using classical binding techniques, including paring and tooling of fine leather. He also creates custom boxes of all sorts.

Though no longer selling my bookbinding services, I continue to pursue the craft as a hobby, creating and restoring books and boxes for friends and family.
