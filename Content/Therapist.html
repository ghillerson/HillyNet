Gary Hillerson, M.A.

Psychotherapist

In 1988, I decided to return to graduate school to study psychology and become a Marriage and Family Therapist, with the intention of helping software engineers to learn how to balance their work and home lives. I graduated in 1991 from Pacifica Graduate Institute, one of the premier Jungian-oriented psychology graduate programs in the United States.

From 1991 until 2008, I worked part-time as a psychotherapist, both with individual clients and with groups. My specialty was helping men to become more aware of their emotional lives. I also ran two longstanding and rewarding groups with high-functioning men who worked in high tech jobs.
